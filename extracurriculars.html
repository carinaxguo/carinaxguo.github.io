Extracurriculars

Debate

I’ve competed in Public Forum debate for seven years. Now, I’m Captain of the Potomac Debate Academy’s 400+ students and was a founder of my school’s national debate team. In debate, I’ve led research efforts, prepared cases, and debated domestic and foreign policy for national circuit tournaments, investigating topics including corporate regulation (e.g. compulsory licensing), federal budgeting (e.g. social spending), and international influence (e.g. military presence in the Persian Gulf).

STEM

Having participated in my schools’ math and computer teams since middle school, I now lead over 150 students as Captain of the RMHS Math Team and Computer Team, where I create weekly presentations, lead team practices for tournaments, and teach members about various mathematical and computer science concepts (AMC 10/12 + AIME for math, and Python and Java for computer science).

DECA

Throughout high school, I participated in my school’s DECA team, choosing different divisions (marketing, finance, business law and ethics) to study a wide range of topics. Now, I’m Executive Vice President of the RMHS team, the second largest club in the school with over 200 members!

Student Goverment

Since freshman year, I’ve been a member of the RMHS Student Government Association’s Executive Board. As a Delegate Advisor, I coordinate representatives for our student body of 2,400 for monthly general assemblies. Previously, I also coded scripts to help streamline processes such as attendance tracking.
